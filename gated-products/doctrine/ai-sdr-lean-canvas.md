# AI SDR Lean Canvas
## 39-Channel Distribution Strategy for AI Sales Products

Stop pretending outreach is just email. The market has fragmented into 39 distinct distribution channels—digital, human-powered, physical, and location-based. Most teams use 3. Leaders use them strategically.

This lean canvas captures the complete go-to-market playbook for an AI-native SDR product (or any sales automation tool). The unique value isn't the channels—it's the *framework* for deciding which ones matter for your specific buyer, the estimated ROI thresholds, and the hybrid model that pairs AI with human gig workers to unlock channels AI alone can't reach.

---

## The Problem

**What your buyers actually face:**

1. **Tool sprawl.** Sales teams use 20+ applications—email, CRM, social scheduling, video platforms, analytics—none talking to each other.
2. **False scarcity of leads.** Even with heavy email campaigns, most startups only generate 30–50 qualified leads per month.
3. **Channel abandonment.** Teams focus on 2–3 channels (email, LinkedIn) and ignore proven channels (YouTube, Reddit, direct mail) because execution feels too manual.
4. **Cost arbitrage failure.** Hiring SDRs costs $60–80K annually; agencies cost $10K+ monthly for strategy alone. Middle-market buyers can't afford either.
5. **Orchestration blindness.** No one coordinates across channels. A lead might see an email *and* a LinkedIn message *and* a TikTok video—or see none of them.

**The deeper insight:** Traditional SDR playbooks are built on the assumption that outreach is mostly synchronous (live phone calls, in-person meetings). But Gen-Z buyers ignore calls and doors. Distribution today is *asynchronous, multi-touch, and cross-platform*. Teams need a framework that treats all 39 channels as equal parts of a single system.

---

## The Solution

**An orchestration platform that:**

- **Finds** prospects across job-change APIs, email databases, intent signals, and community platforms
- **Creates** channel-native content: email copy, TikTok scripts, Reddit comments, direct-mail letters, voicemail copy
- **Distributes** across all 39 channels simultaneously, with deduplication and sequencing rules
- **Manages** the human layer: AI books gig workers (for door-to-door, flyers, direct mail, voicemail recording) and monitors their output for quality
- **Learns** which channels, messaging angles, and sequencing rules work best per vertical, then auto-scales winners

The product isn't just a distribution engine. It's a teaching system. Over 90 days of use, the AI learns that lawyers respond best to direct mail + LinkedIn + Substack, while SaaS founders respond to Twitter + YouTube + TikTok. It then reweights the channel strategy for each customer's vertical.

## Implementation Framework: Channel Strategy by Buyer Size

### Bootstrapped (0–$500K ARR)
**Channels:** 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 (Email, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, Reddit)
**Human layer:** None initially. Test channel fit first.
**Estimated monthly leads:** 20–40
**Cost:** $99–299/month subscription

### Growth Stage ($500K–$5M ARR)
**Channels:** 1–7, 20, 21, 22 (Add YouTube, Discord, cold calling via gig worker, InMail)
**Human layer:** Start here. Hire 1–2 gig workers for cold calling, InMail follow-up.
**Estimated monthly leads:** 40–150
**Cost:** $299–799/month + human layer costs ($500–2K/month)

### Scale-up ($5M–$50M ARR)
**Channels:** 1–20, 21–25, 29 (Add voicemail, personalised video, direct mail)
**Human layer:** 3–5 gig workers, account-based everything for top 100 prospects.
**Estimated monthly leads:** 150–500
**Cost:** $799–1,499/month + human ($2K–5K/month)

### Enterprise ($50M+ ARR)
**Channels:** All except 36–39 (tailored per geography)
**Human layer:** Full operationalisation—call teams, event staffing, hand-delivered gifts for key accounts.
**Estimated monthly leads:** 500–2,000+
**Cost:** Custom, $5K–20K+/month

## The Channel Selection Heuristic

When launching a new product or vertical, prioritise channels using this sequence:

1. **Baseline (Days 1–30):** Email, LinkedIn, Twitter/X. Test messaging, measure reply rate, identify early signals.
2. **Add video (Days 31–60):** Layer in TikTok or YouTube Shorts with AI-generated or founder-recorded content. Video engagement typically outperforms text 3–5x.
3. **Community expansion (Days 61–90):** Activate Reddit, Discord, or niche Slack communities where your buyer congregates. Avoid cold pitching; participate first.
4. **Human layer (Days 91–120):** Test gig workers for cold calling, voicemail, or InMail. Measure conversion lift. If >20% improvement, scale.
5. **Physical (120+):** Only if you've validated email + digital + human layer. Physical is expensive; use it to reinforce, not to explore.

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Channels deployed** (per customer)—how many are active, not just licensed
2. **Leads generated** (per channel, per vertical)—disaggregate by source, not aggregate
3. **Cost per lead** vs. traditional (SDR hire, agency) by channel
4. **Conversion rate** from outreach to MQL/SQL, by channel
5. **Vertical PMF**—which channels + messaging combos work best for lawyers vs. founders vs. enterprises
6. **Human layer ROI**—does adding gig workers improve conversion by ≥15%?

## Practitioner's Note: When to Use This Framework

This 39-channel model works for:
- **SaaS products** with diverse buyer personas (founders, operators, CTOs, CFOs)
- **AI tools** where distribution is your moat (your customers care about reach, not features alone)
- **Vertical SaaS** where you can afford to specialise (one model per vertical)
- **High-ticket sales** ($10K+ ACV) where small conversion improvements mean $1M+ impact

It's overkill for:
- Consumer products (focus on channels 4, 6, 17, 27)
- Marketplace platforms (focus on channels 2, 6, 11, 12)
- Gaming (focus on channels 4, 12, 38)

## Next Steps for Builders

1. **Identify your buyer.** Which of the 32 customer segments do you serve?
2. **Pick your MVP channels** from the baseline set (email, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube).
3. **Measure channel fit.** Run 500–1,000 touches per channel, measure reply rate and conversion.
4. **Add video.** If text reply rate is <5%, add TikTok/YouTube Shorts. Video typically unlocks 12–18% reply.
5. **Test human layer.** Run 100 cold calls via gig worker, measure close rate vs. email baseline.
6. **Expand geographically or vertically.** Once one segment is validated, repeat the framework for the next.
