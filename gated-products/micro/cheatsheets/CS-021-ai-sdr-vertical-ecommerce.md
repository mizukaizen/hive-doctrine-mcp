# AI SDR Vertical: E-Commerce — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

An e-commerce-specific playbook for AI SDR outreach. Lifecycle-based segmentation, seasonality windows, and messaging templates by store size.

## Lifecycle-Based Customer Segmentation

| Segment | Revenue | Key Characteristic | Messaging Angle |
|---------|---------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Micro** | <$100K/yr | Solo founder, Shopify | "Stop doing outreach manually" |
| **Small** | $100K-$1M/yr | Small team, growing | "Scale without hiring a sales team" |
| **Mid-Market** | $1M-$10M/yr | Established, multi-channel | "Personalise at scale" |
| **Enterprise** | $10M+/yr | Large team, complex stack | "Integrate with your existing CRM" |

## Seasonality Windows

| Window | Timing | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Pre-Q4** | Aug-Sep | Biggest buying season prep |
| **Post-Holiday** | Jan-Feb | Budget planning, tool evaluation |
| **Pre-Summer** | Apr-May | Mid-year campaigns |
| **Back-to-School** | Jun-Jul | Second-largest retail window |

**Rule:** Outreach 6-8 weeks before the seasonal window. Too late = already committed to tools.

## 3 Messaging Templates

**Micro:** "You're spending 5 hours/week on outreach that converts at 1%. What if AI wrote emails that convert at 5%? Try it for $49/mo."

**Mid-Market:** "Your team sends 2,000 emails/month. Each one takes 8 minutes to personalise. That's 267 hours/month. We cut it to 20."

**Enterprise:** "Your sales ops team manages 15 tools. We integrate with all of them and add AI personalisation on top."
