# AI SDR Vertical: SaaS — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

A SaaS-specific playbook for AI SDR outreach. Covers segmentation, channel sequencing, and messaging by company stage.

## 5-Segment Targeting

| Segment | ARR | Eval Window | Deal Size | Decision Maker |
|---------|-----|-------------|-----------|----------------|
| **Bootstrapped** | <$500K | 1-2 weeks | $49-149/mo | Founder |
| **Seed** | $500K-$2M | 2-4 weeks | $149-299/mo | Head of Growth |
| **Series A** | $2M-$10M | 3-6 weeks | $299-499/mo | VP Sales |
| **Series B+** | $10M-$50M | 4-8 weeks | $499-999/mo | Director of Sales |
| **Enterprise** | $50M+ | 8-16 weeks | $1K+/mo | CRO/SVP Sales |

## Technographic + Funding Intelligence

Use these signals to prioritise outreach:

- **Just raised funding** → Likely hiring sales, need to scale pipeline
- **Using competitor tools** → Active buyer, knows the category
- **Tech stack signals** → Salesforce = enterprise ready; HubSpot = mid-market
- **Job postings for SDRs** → They're scaling outbound = perfect timing

## Channel Sequencing

| Step | Channel | Timing |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | **Email** (personalised) | Day 1 |
| 2 | **LinkedIn** (connect + note) | Day 3 |
| 3 | **Twitter/X** (engage with their content) | Day 5 |
| 4 | **Email** (follow-up, new angle) | Day 7 |
| 5 | **LinkedIn** (voice memo) | Day 10 |
| 6 | **Cold Call** (if enterprise) | Day 14 |

## Messaging by Segment

**Bootstrapped:** "You're doing outreach yourself. What if your AI did it while you built product?"

**Series A:** "Your first SDR hire costs $60K+. Test AI outreach for $149/mo first."

**Enterprise:** "Your team sends 10,000 emails/month. What if each one was genuinely personalised?"

## 6-Week Launch Timeline

- Week 1: Build prospect list (500 per segment)
- Week 2: Write and test email sequences
- Week 3: Launch to Bootstrapped + Seed segments
- Week 4: Launch to Series A segment
- Week 5: Analyse results, optimise messaging
- Week 6: Scale winning sequences, add LinkedIn channel
