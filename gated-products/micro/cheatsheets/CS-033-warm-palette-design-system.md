# Warm Palette Design System — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

Complete design token definitions for AI-native products. Warm, opinionated, alive — designed for products where clarity and intelligence matter more than trends.

## 3 Identity Principles

1. **Information density with breathing room** — accommodate complex data without claustrophobia
2. **Warmth without sentimentality** — human-scale, not sterile or saccharine
3. **Intelligence over decoration** — every visual element serves comprehension

## Colour Tokens

|-------|-------|-----|
| `--bg-primary` | `#FAFAF8` | Page background (warm off-white) |
| `--bg-secondary` | `#F2F0EC` | Card/section backgrounds |
| `--text-primary` | `#1A1918` | Body text (warm black) |
| `--text-secondary` | `#6B6966` | Secondary text |
