# AI SDR Go-to-Market — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

A 4-phase GTM plan for launching an AI SDR product, from foundation to scale.

## 4 Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
- Build landing page with waitlist
- Define ICP: bootstrapped SaaS founders, $10K-$100K MRR
- Content marketing: 2 blog posts/week on cold outreach
- Community presence: Indie Hackers, SaaS Twitter, LinkedIn
- **Goal:** 500 waitlist signups

### Phase 2: Beta to Paid (Weeks 5-8)
- Invite top 50 waitlist users to beta
- Offer beta pricing: 50% off for 6 months
- Weekly feedback calls with 5 beta users
- Fix critical bugs, ship top 3 feature requests
- **Goal:** 20 paying customers at beta pricing

### Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 9-12)
- Launch on Product Hunt
- Start paid ads (Google Ads: "AI sales outreach", LinkedIn Ads targeting sales managers)
- Implement referral program (1 month free per referral)
- Case studies from beta customers
- **Goal:** 100 paying customers

### Phase 4: Expansion (Months 4-6)
- Add enterprise tier ($499+/mo)
- Sales team (1 AE) for enterprise deals
- Partner channel (CRM integrations, agency partnerships)
- **Goal:** $50K MRR

## Risk Register

|------|--------|------------|
| Email deliverability issues | HIGH | Warm-up domains, use reputable ESPs |
