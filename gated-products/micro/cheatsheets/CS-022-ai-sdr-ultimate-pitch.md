# AI SDR Ultimate Pitch — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

A pitch framework for AI SDR products. Covers hybrid vs pure autonomous models and The Ultimate Sequence (6-touch multi-channel outreach).

## Hybrid vs Pure Autonomous

| Model | Human Role | Churn | Best For |
|-------|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Pure Autonomous** | None — AI does everything | 50-80% | Low-value, high-volume leads |
| **Hybrid** | AI drafts, human reviews/sends | 20-35% | Mid-to-high-value leads |

**Key insight:** Pure autonomous AI SDRs churn at 50-80% because buyers detect robotic outreach. Hybrid models retain because a human catches tone-deaf messages before they send.

## The Ultimate Sequence (6 Touches)

| Touch | Channel | Content | Timing |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| 1 | **Personalised Video** | 60-sec Loom referencing their product/company | Day 1 |
| 2 | **Dynamic ROI PDF** | Auto-generated with their company data | Day 3 |
| 3 | **LinkedIn Voice Memo** | 30-sec audio, casual tone | Day 5 |
| 4 | **Real-Time News Hook** | Email referencing their recent announcement | Day 7 |
| 5 | **Tone-Matched Email** | Writing style matched to their own content | Day 10 |
| 6 | **Dynamic Landing Page** | Personalised page with their logo and use case | Day 14 |

**Why it works:** Each touch uses a different medium and a different personalisation signal. The prospect feels individually targeted because they are.
