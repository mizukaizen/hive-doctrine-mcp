# Linear Workflow Philosophy — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

Data showing linear workflows beat autonomous agents for production AI products. Positioning AI as leverage (not replacement) drives 85%+ retention vs 35-50% for autonomous agents.

## The Core Insight

| Approach | Retention | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| **Linear Workflows** (AI assists human steps) | 85%+ | Predictable, debuggable, human stays in control |
| **Autonomous Agents** (AI runs independently) | 35-50% | Unpredictable failures, trust erosion, hard to debug |

**Rule:** Build linear workflows first. Add autonomy only where the failure cost is near zero.

## Positioning: Leverage, Not Replacement

**Wrong:** "Our AI replaces your [role]"
**Right:** "Our AI makes your [role] 5x more productive"

Replacement positioning scares buyers (job threat) and sets impossible expectations. Leverage positioning makes the buyer feel empowered and sets achievable expectations.
