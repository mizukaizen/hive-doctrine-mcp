# Willingness to Pay Framework — Cheat Sheet

## What It Is

A $10K, 14-day framework that tests whether people will actually pay for your solution before you build it. Money is the truth serum.

## Core Insight

Problem awareness does not equal willingness to pay. "Do you have this problem?" (100% yes) is a different question from "Will you pay $99/month for this solution?" (maybe 2% yes).

## 5 Phases

### Phase 1: Hypothesis Definition (Days 1-2)
Define in one sentence each:
- **ICP:** Who specifically (not "everyone")
- **Problem:** What specific pain
- **Solution:** The MVP offering
- **Positioning:** How you pitch it
- **Price:** Test price point

### Phase 2: Landing Page + Ad Setup (Days 3-4)
- Build validation page: problem, solution, CTA
- CTA options: "Get early access — $99/mo" or "Enrol — $297"
- Set up $1,500-2,000 in Google/LinkedIn Ads targeting ICP

### Phase 3: Run Traffic (Days 5-7)
Spend $1,500. Track clicks, signups, payments.

| Conversion | Verdict |
|------------|---------|
| >5% payment (or >15% email) | ICP confirmed. Proceed. |
| 1-5% payment (or 5-15% email) | Reposition and retest |
| <1% payment (or <5% email) | Kill this ICP |

### Phase 4: Packaging Variants (Days 8-10)
Test 3-5 messaging angles on the same product. $500 ads each.

| Result | Decision |
|--------|----------|
| One variant 2x higher than others | Clear winner — use that positioning |
| All variants similar | Problem-solution fit is the lever, not messaging |
| All variants <3% | ICP isn't viable |

### Phase 5: Price Sensitivity (Days 11-14)
Test 3 price points. $500 ads each.

| Result | Decision |
|--------|----------|
| Conversion drops <50% at higher price | Price elasticity is low — charge more |
| Conversion drops >50% | Stay at lower price |
| >2% conversion at highest price | You're underpriced |

## Go/No-Go Criteria

- **Go:** >5% payment, one packaging variant wins 2x, >3% at target price
- **Refine:** 2-5% payment, reposition and retest (max 3 cycles)
- **Kill:** <1% payment across all ICPs tested

## Total Cost: ~$8-10K

Landing page ($200-500) + Ad spend ($7-8K) + Tools ($200-500) + 20 hours of your time.
